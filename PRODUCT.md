# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Recruiters, hiring managers, and potential freelance clients evaluating Softjit Singh's skills, projects, and experience. Each arrives with a different lens: recruiters scan for technical fit and role readiness; clients evaluate aesthetic quality and execution.

## Product Purpose

A personal portfolio that lands job opportunities and freelance work by demonstrating full-stack and mobile engineering capability through real projects. Success is measured by interview requests and client inquiries.

## Brand Personality

Creative, energetic, playful. The tone is confident but never arrogant, polished but never stiff. The portfolio should feel like meeting someone who genuinely loves building things.

## Anti-references

Over-animated or flashy portfolios that prioritize gimmicks over substance. No gratuitous motion, no effects that exist only to impress. The energy should come from the work itself.

## Design Principles

- **Confident creativity** — Creative and energetic, but never try-hard or gimmicky. Playfulness lives in thoughtful details, not flashy effects.
- **Show, don't tell** — Projects and skills are the star. Every design decision helps the work speak for itself.
- **Dual-purpose clarity** — The same page must resonate with technical recruiters scanning for skills and clients evaluating visual quality.
- **Playful restraint** — Motion serves understanding, not spectacle. Energetic but never over-animated or flashy.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA compliance for color contrast. Supports reduced motion preferences. No critical information conveyed through color alone.
